The \mbox{\hyperlink{a00954}{IMusic}} class. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{a00954}{IMusic}} class is the class where you can manipulate Sounds and Musics. ATTENTION\+: This class is of Comp\+Type MUSIC ATTENTION\+: You must have a parameter in your constructor in order to set his id.
